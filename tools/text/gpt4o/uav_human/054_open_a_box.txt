
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'open a box' action in a sentence.
Strictly follow these rules:
1. No lists, bullet points, or dashes
2. No line breaks
3. Keep descriptions brief
4. Start directly with the description

Round 1 Answer:
The human body bends slightly at the waist, reaches forward with one or both hands to grasp the box lid, then lifts or slides it open while stabilizing the box with the other hand if needed.
