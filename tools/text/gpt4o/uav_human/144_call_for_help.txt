
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'call for help' action in a sentence.
Strictly follow these rules:
1. No lists, bullet points, or dashes
2. No line breaks
3. Keep descriptions brief
4. Start directly with the description

Round 1 Answer:
The human body performs the 'call for help' action by raising one or both arms vigorously above the head, often while waving or shaking them, and may simultaneously shout or vocalize loudly to attract attention.
