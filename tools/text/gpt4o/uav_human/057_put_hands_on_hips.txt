
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'put hands on hips' action in a sentence.
Strictly follow these rules:
1. No lists, bullet points, or dashes
2. No line breaks
3. Keep descriptions brief
4. Start directly with the description

Round 1 Answer:
The arms bend at the elbows as the hands move upward and outward, placing the palms on the hips with fingers pointing forward and thumbs resting backward.
