
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'put something into a bag' action in a sentence.
Strictly follow these rules:
1. No lists, bullet points, or dashes
2. No line breaks
3. Keep descriptions brief
4. Start directly with the description

Round 1 Answer:
The hand reaches toward the object, grasps it firmly, lifts it while the other hand opens the bag, then moves the object inside and releases it before withdrawing.
