
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'take off a shoe' action in a sentence.

Round 1 Answer:
The human body performs the 'take off a shoe' action by bending at the waist or hips, reaching down with one or both hands to grasp the shoe, loosening or pulling it off the foot, and then straightening back up.
