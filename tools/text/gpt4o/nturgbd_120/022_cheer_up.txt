
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'cheer up' action in a sentence.

Round 1 Answer:
The human body performs the 'cheer up' action by lifting the arms upward with palms open, often accompanied by a slight jump, a bright facial expression, and an energetic vocalization like a cheer or shout.
