
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'make victory sign' action in a sentence.

Round 1 Answer:
The human body performs the 'make victory sign' action by raising one arm, bending the elbow slightly, and extending the index and middle fingers upward while keeping the other fingers curled into the palm.
