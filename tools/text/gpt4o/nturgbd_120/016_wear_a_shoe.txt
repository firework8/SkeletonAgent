
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'wear a shoe' action in a sentence.

Round 1 Answer:
The human body performs the 'wear a shoe' action by bending at the waist and knees, lifting one foot, guiding the toes into the shoe's opening, and sliding the foot forward until the heel settles into place.

Round 2 Answer:
