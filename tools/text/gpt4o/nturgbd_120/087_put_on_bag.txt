
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'put on bag' action in a sentence.

Round 1 Answer:
The human body performs the 'put on bag' action by lifting the arm, sliding the hand through the bag's strap, and adjusting the strap over the shoulder while maintaining balance.
