
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'flick hair' action in a sentence.

Round 1 Answer:
The 'flick hair' action involves a quick, upward or sideways movement of the head, often combined with a hand brushing through the hair to toss it back or to the side.
