
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'take off bag' action in a sentence.

Round 1 Answer:
The human body performs the 'take off bag' action by bending slightly at the waist, lifting the arms to grasp the bag's straps or handles, and then smoothly pulling it upward and away from the torso while shifting balance slightly backward to maintain stability.
