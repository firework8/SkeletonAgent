
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'grasp a bag' action in a sentence.
Strictly follow these rules:
1. No lists, bullet points, or dashes
2. No line breaks
3. Keep descriptions brief
4. Start directly with the description

Round 1 Answer:

Round 2 Question:

Strictly follow these rules:
1. Each body part must be listed separately in this exact order: Head, Hand, Arm, Hip, Leg, Foot
2. Format as "[Body part]: [Description]." with no dashes or numbers
3. Use exactly one line per body part with no blank lines
4. Never combine body parts (e.g., no "Hand & Arm")
5. Keep descriptions brief

Round 2 Answer:
Head: Remains stable or slightly tilts to observe the bag.  
Hand: Opens, reaches, then closes around the bag's handle.  
Arm: Extends forward, bends at the elbow to pull the bag closer.  
Hip: May shift slightly for balance but stays mostly still.  
Leg: Bears weight evenly with minor adjustments for stability.  
Foot: Stays planted on the ground to maintain posture.
