
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'step on the spot walk' action in a sentence.
Strictly follow these rules:
1. No lists, bullet points, or dashes
2. No line breaks
3. Keep descriptions brief
4. Start directly with the description

Round 1 Answer:
The human body performs the 'step on the spot walk' by alternately lifting each knee slightly while swinging the opposite arm forward, maintaining an upright posture with minimal forward movement, and landing each foot softly in place.
