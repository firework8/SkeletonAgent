
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'make an OK sign' action in a sentence.
Strictly follow these rules:
1. No lists, bullet points, or dashes
2. No line breaks
3. Keep descriptions brief
4. Start directly with the description

Round 1 Answer:
The hand moves to form a circle by touching the tip of the thumb to the tip of the index finger while the other fingers remain extended or slightly curled.
