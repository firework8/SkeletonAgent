
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'cross palms together' action in a sentence.
Strictly follow these rules:
1. No lists, bullet points, or dashes
2. No line breaks
3. Keep descriptions brief
4. Start directly with the description

Round 1 Answer:
The human body performs the 'cross palms together' action by moving both arms inward from the sides, rotating the wrists to face each other, and bringing the palms to meet in front of the torso with fingers aligned and hands overlapping slightly.
