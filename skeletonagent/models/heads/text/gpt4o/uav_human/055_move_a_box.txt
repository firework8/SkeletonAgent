
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'move a box' action in a sentence.
Strictly follow these rules:
1. No lists, bullet points, or dashes
2. No line breaks
3. Keep descriptions brief
4. Start directly with the description

Round 1 Answer:
The human body bends at the knees and hips to lower the torso, grasps the box with both hands, then straightens the legs and back while lifting the box, followed by walking to the desired location and bending again to place it down.
