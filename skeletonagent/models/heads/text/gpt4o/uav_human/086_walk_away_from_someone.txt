
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'walk away from someone' action in a sentence.
Strictly follow these rules:
1. No lists, bullet points, or dashes
2. No line breaks
3. Keep descriptions brief
4. Start directly with the description

Round 1 Answer:
The person shifts their weight onto one leg, lifts the other foot, steps forward, and repeats the motion while turning their body away from the other individual, gradually increasing distance.
