
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'tennis bat swing' action in a sentence.

Round 1 Answer:
The tennis bat swing involves a coordinated sequence where the player rotates their torso, shifts weight from the back to the front foot, swings the arm forward with a hinged wrist, and follows through to generate power and accuracy in the shot.
