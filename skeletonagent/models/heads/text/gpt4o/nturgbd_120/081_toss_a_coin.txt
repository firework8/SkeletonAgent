
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'toss a coin' action in a sentence.

Round 1 Answer:
When tossing a coin, the human body engages the forearm and wrist in a quick upward flick, propelling the coin into the air with a controlled spin before catching or letting it land.
