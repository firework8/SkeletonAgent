
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'hush (quite)' action in a sentence.

Round 1 Answer:
The human body performs the 'hush (quiet)' action by bringing a finger (usually the index) vertically to the lips while slightly tilting the head forward, often accompanied by a soft "shh" sound to signal silence.
