
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'pat on back of other person' action in a sentence.

Round 1 Answer:
The person raises their arm, bends their elbow to bring their hand toward the other person's back, and gently moves their hand downward in a light tapping or rubbing motion to complete the pat.
