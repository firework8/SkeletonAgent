
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'point finger at the other person' action in a sentence.

Round 1 Answer:
The human body performs the 'point finger at the other person' action by extending the arm outward from the shoulder, straightening the elbow, and using the index finger to direct attention toward the target while keeping the other fingers curled into the palm.
