
Round 1 Question:
Describe the movement process of the human body performing the 'pointing to something with finger' action in a sentence.

Round 1 Answer:
The human body performs the 'pointing to something with finger' action by extending the arm, straightening the elbow, and aligning the index finger toward the target while the other fingers remain curled or relaxed.

Round 2 Answer:

Foot: Maintains contact with the floor, toes facing forward.
